Discover my background, journey, and passion for data analysis

Professional Journey: I embarked on my professional journey in the world of data analysis with a strong educational foundation. I earned an MSc in Accounting and Finance from the University of Leicester and graduated with first-class honours in Business Administration from Babcock University in Nigeria. These academic achievements laid the groundwork for my career in the field of data and intelligence.

Mission and Vision: I aim to decipher the stories hidden within data and transform them into actionable insights. I firmly believe that data is not merely a collection of numbers; it's the key to unlocking strategic decisions. I also view Intelligence as the lens through which we make sense of the world. This vision guides my work and fuels my passion for data analysis.

Professional Accomplishments: Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with the Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit (NFIU), where I've produced insightful reports for local organizations and financial intelligence units. One of my notable achievements was drafting a comprehensive report on the Nigerian currency redesign policy of 2022, which informed the Central Bank of Nigeria's decisions. I've also supported and completed two Egmont group's projects on the Use of Open-Source Intelligence, and the abuse of NPOs for terrorist financing purposes. Apart from occasionally optimizing data extraction, building visualisation dashboards and making meaning of raw data, you may also catch me identifying financial crime patterns and producing insightful reports out of them to help my country catch the bad guys :).

Recognition: In 2022, I was honoured to receive the 'Most Innovative Intelligence Analyst' award from the Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit. Additionally, my academic excellence was recognised with the 'Leicester Award Gold' from the University of Leicester in 2019.

What Drives Me: I'm driven by the endless possibilities that data analysis offers. It's not just a job; it's a lifelong pursuit of knowledge, innovation, and making a meaningful impact. I'm eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore new ways to leverage data and intelligence. Let's join forces and dive deeper into the world of data analysis together. Feel free to browse my portfolio, and if you have any questions or opportunities to discuss, don't hesitate to get in touch. I'm always open to new collaborations and challenges. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you.
